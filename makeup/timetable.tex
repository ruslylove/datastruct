\section*{Ground Rules (Reverse Classroom)}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Bring \textbf{written attempts} of the pre-class exercises to every session, even if incomplete --- ``stuck'' is useful information.
  \item Sessions focus on \textbf{your work first}, mini-lectures only where confusion is shared.
  \item Late or missing pre-class work means the session becomes lecture-only, which we both want to avoid.
  \item The final exam is comprehensive and weighted toward the kinds of problems found in the pre-class exercises.
\end{itemize}
